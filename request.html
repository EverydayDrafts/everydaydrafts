Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for you to deliver the finished product?

After you submit your request, we start working on your project right away. However, before we start working on your project we need to do some research to better understand your business, your products, services, history, competitors, industry, and more. The research usually takes 7-8 days, once we are done with research, we start working on your brand design. At Opulence, we use "design-review-correct-repeat" formula, generally a brand design can take anywhere between 30-90 days. However, our average deliver times are 45 days.

What is your's refund policy?

If for any reason you are not satisfied with our work, you can request a refund. However, once the designers have started working on your project, you will only be refunded 50% of the total payment you made. The designers usually starts working on any project in 7-8 days, thus we have restricted full refunds to be valid only to the first 7 days after you submit your request.

When and where we are launching our full stack of services?

We are expected to launch our services in multiple stages in next following months. Our services will be open to everyone around the world at same prices. However, few services including incorporation paperwork and opening bank accounts will be limited to handful of countries including United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Germany, Sweden, Norway, Switzerland, India, China, Singapore and Australia. We are working to extent this list and will let you know when it happens.

How can I contact you?

The best and quickest way to reach us is by using the hovering chat tab in the right bottom part of this page. You can also write us an email at contact@everydaydrafts.com. Usually we reply in less than 48 hours but time can vary depending on customer support requests.
